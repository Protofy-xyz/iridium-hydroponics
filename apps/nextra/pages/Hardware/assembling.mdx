<div>
Note that the water reservoir should be covered to prevent light entry. Light exposure can promote the growth of algae and pathogens, leading to various issues. Sunlight can also increase the temperature of the water in the reservoir.

Several components are essential inside the water reservoir: the main flow pump, EC & pH probes, float switch, silicone tubes, etc.

For this reason, we have designed a laser-cut add-on to cover the reservoir. This add-on attaches to the perimeter of the reservoir‚Äôs open face, securing and organizing all the elements that go inside. It features a removable lid that allows access to the interior of the reservoir without interfering with the installed components.
</div>
